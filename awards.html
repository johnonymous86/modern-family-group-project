Awards

Over 11 seasons and over 124 awards!


Modern Family ran during Prime Time on ABC for 11 seasons. During that run, the show was honored with numerous industry awards, including:


Primetime Emmy
  • Outstanding Comedy Series (2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014)
  • Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series: Julie Bowen (2011, 2012)
  • Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series: Eric Stonestreet (2010, 2012); Ty Burrell (2011, 2014)
Golden Globes
  • Best Television Series (2012)
Peabody Awards
  • Winner 2010
People's Choice awards
  • Sofia Vergara (Favorite Comedic Actress: 2017, The Comedy TV Star of 2020: 2020)
Screen Actors Guild Awards
  • Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Comedy Series (2011, 2012, 2013, 2014)
  • Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Comedy Series: Ty Burrell (2014)
Writer's Guild of America Awards
  • 2010: New Series - Television; Episodic Comedy - Television (Steven Levitan and Christopher Lloyd for "Pilot")
  • 2011: Comedy Series - Television
  • 2012: Comedy Series - Television; Episodic Comedy - Television (Steven Levitan and Jeffrey Richman for "Caught in the Act")
  • 2013: Episodic Comedy - Television (Elaine Ko for "Virgin Territory")
