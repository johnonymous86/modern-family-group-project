Meet the Family

Jay Pritchett

The family patriarch. Jay is blunt, sarcastic, and deeply caring—though he doesn't always show it.

Gloria Pritchett

Jay's energetic and passionate Colombian wife with a big heart and a loud voice to match.

Manny Delgado

Gloria's son. A romantic old soul who prefers poetry and espresso to video games.

Joe Pritchett

The youngest Pritchett. Full of curiosity and unpredictability.

Mitchell Pritchett

Jay's son. A smart, tightly wound lawyer who's always trying to prove himself.

Cameron Tucker

Mitchell's husband. Dramatic, hilarious, and endlessly warm-hearted.

Lily Tucker-Pritchett

Lily is Mitchell and Cameron's adopted daughter. She's witty and growing up fast in a very modern household.

Phil Dunphy

A goofy, lovable dad who tries way too hard to be cool—and kinda is.

Claire Dunphy

Phil's wife. Type-A, supermom, and the backbone of the Dunphy household.

Haley Dunphy

Claire and Phil's oldest. Fashion-loving and full of surprises.

Alex Dunphy

The genius middle child. Quiet, sarcastic, and always a few steps ahead.

Luke Dunphy

The youngest Dunphy. Fun-loving and sometimes... a little clueless.
